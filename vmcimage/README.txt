🚀 HOW TO USE:
1. LOCAL VIEWING:
   - Double-click "index.html" to open in browser
   - All student photos will display automatically

2. UPLOAD TO WEB HOSTING:
   Option A - Upload as-is:
   - Upload all files to your web host
   - Visit your domain to see the live site

   Option B - Rename to your domain:
   - Upload to public_html/ or www/ folder
   - Ensure images/ folder is also uploaded

3. REPLACE IMAGES (if needed):
   - Put new photos in the "images/" folder
   - Keep same filenames OR update HTML
